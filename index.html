Chicago, IL

On May 2, 1978 I was born in Chicago, Il to the parents of Rein and Sherry Turn. I lived in Chicago for about two years. Naturally I became a diehard Cubs fan.

Davenport, IA

At age two my parents moved our family to Davenport, IA. Here I was able to solidify my love for the Cubs.

St. Louis, MO

At age four my parents moved our family to St. Louis, MO. I lived in St. Louis until I was 15. During this time I grew to hate the St. Louis Cardinals.

New Milford, CT

At age 15 my parents moved our family to New Milford, CT. I lived in New Milford for 1 and a half years. During this time I learned to hate winter.

St. Louis, MO

Halfway through my junior year of high school my parents moved our family back to St. Louis, MO.

Columbia, MO

At age 18 I graduated from Parkway South High School. In the fall I attended the Univeristy of Missouri - Columbia. I graduated from the University of Missouri in 2000 with a degree in hotel and restaurant management.

St. Louis, MO

Upon graduating from the University of Missouri I returned to St. Louis, MO.
